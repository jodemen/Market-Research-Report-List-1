<p><p>Ultraviolet lamps market can be categorized into two main types based on the material used for the lamp: High Boron Glass Lamp and Quartz Glass Lamp. High Boron Glass Lamps are known for their high UV transmittance and are commonly used in applications where a broad UV spectrum is required. On the other hand, Quartz Glass Lamps are preferred for their superior resistance to high temperatures and chemicals, making them suitable for more demanding applications. Both types of lamps offer different advantages and cater to different market needs.</p></p>
