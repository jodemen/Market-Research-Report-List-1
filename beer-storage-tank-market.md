<p><p>Beer storage tanks come in two main types: vertical and horizontal. Vertical tanks are designed to stand upright, taking up less floor space and allowing for easy access to the top of the tank for cleaning and maintenance. Horizontal tanks, on the other hand, lie on their side and are often used when floor space is limited. They typically have a larger diameter than vertical tanks, providing more surface area for cooling and temperature control. Both types offer different advantages depending on the brewery's needs.</p></p>
