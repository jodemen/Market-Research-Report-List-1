<p><p>Disc Tube Reverse Osmosis (DTRO) and Disc Tube Nano Filtration (DTNF) are two types of membrane technologies used in water treatment processes. DTRO membranes are designed for higher salt rejection and fouling resistance, making them ideal for brackish water treatment. On the other hand, DTNF membranes are used for removing smaller particles and molecules, making them suitable for processes like desalination and wastewater treatment. Both types of membranes offer efficient filtration solutions for various applications in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and municipal water treatment.</p></p>
