<p><p>PTFE Composite Film comes in two main types: Hydrophobic and Hydrophilic. Hydrophobic PTFE Composite Film repels water and other liquids, making it ideal for applications where moisture resistance is essential. On the other hand, Hydrophilic PTFE Composite Film attracts and absorbs water, making it suitable for use in applications where moisture absorption is required. Both types offer unique properties that cater to different needs in various industries such as medical, electronics, and packaging.</p></p>
