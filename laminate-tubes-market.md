<p><strong>The Laminate Tubes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plastic Barrier Laminate (PBL) Tubes</li><li>Aluminum Barrier Laminate (ABL) Tubes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Laminate tubes market includes two main types: Plastic Barrier Laminate (PBL) Tubes and Aluminum Barrier Laminate (ABL) Tubes. PBL tubes are made of layers of plastic materials that provide a barrier against moisture and air. ABL tubes, on the other hand, are made of layers of aluminum and plastic, offering a superior barrier against light, moisture, and air. Both types are widely used in industries such as cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and food for packaging products that require protection and preservation.</p></p>
