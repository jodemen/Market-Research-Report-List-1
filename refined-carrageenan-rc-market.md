There are three main types of RC - Kappa Carrageenan, Iota Carrageenan, and Lambda Carrageenan. Kappa Carrageenan is commonly used in dairy and meat products for its gelling properties. Iota Carrageenan is used for its thickening and stabilizing properties in dairy products and desserts. Lambda Carrageenan is used as a thickener in products like ice cream and salad dressings.</p></p>
