<p><strong>The Panel PC Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fan-Enabled Panel PC</li><li>Fan-Less Panel PC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Panel PC market can be categorized into two types: Fan-Enabled Panel PC and Fan-Less Panel PC. A Fan-Enabled Panel PC comprises a cooling fan to dissipate heat generated by the internal components, ensuring optimal performance. On the other hand, a Fan-Less Panel PC uses passive cooling methods like heat sinks, relying on natural air circulation to keep the system cool. Both types have their advantages; while Fan-Enabled PCs offer better cooling efficiency, Fan-Less PCs are more silent, require less maintenance, and are resistant to dust and dirt.</p></p>
