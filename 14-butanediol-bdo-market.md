<p><p>1,4 Butanediol (BDO) is a versatile chemical used in various industries. The market types for BDO production include the Reppe process, Davy process, Butadiene process, and Propylene oxide process. The Reppe process involves catalytic hydrogenation of maleic anhydride, while the Davy process utilizes the catalytic hydrogenation of succinic acid. The Butadiene process involves the hydroformylation of butadiene, and the Propylene oxide process involves the hydroformylation of propylene. Each process has its own advantages and applications in the production of BDO.</p></p>
