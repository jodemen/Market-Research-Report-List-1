<p><strong>The Cloud IT Infrastructure Services and Solutions Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Public Cloud</li><li>Private Cloud</li><li>Hybrid Cloud</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cloud IT infrastructure services and solutions are classified into three main market types: public cloud, private cloud, and hybrid cloud.</p><p>Public Cloud: This type of market refers to services offered over the internet by third-party providers to multiple organizations or individuals. These services are available to the public and shared among various users.</p><p>Private Cloud: In this market, services and infrastructure are exclusively used by a single organization. The private cloud can either be hosted on-premises or provided by a third-party provider, ensuring higher security and customization options.</p><p>Hybrid Cloud: This market combines the benefits of both public and private clouds. Organizations can use a mix of private and public cloud services while maintaining data and application portability between them. This allows businesses to optimize cost and efficiency, ensuring sensitive data is kept secure in the private cloud while leveraging the scalability and flexibility of the public cloud.</p></p>
