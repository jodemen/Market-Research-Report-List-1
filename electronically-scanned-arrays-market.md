<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronically Scanned Arrays (ESAs) are used in various applications, such as radar systems, communication systems, and satellite systems. In the market, there are two types of ESAs: active and passive. Active ESAs feature integrated transmit and receive modules, allowing for simultaneous transmission and reception of signals. They are suitable for applications requiring high power and long-range detection. On the other hand, passive ESAs rely on receiving signals from other sources and do not have their own transmit capabilities. They are commonly used in electronic warfare and signal monitoring applications.</p></p>
