<p><p>Maracuja oil is extracted from passion fruit seeds using two primary methods: cold-pressed and hot-pressed. Cold-pressed maracuja oil is obtained by applying pressure to the seeds at low temperatures, ensuring the preservation of nutrients and natural properties. On the other hand, hot-pressed maracuja oil involves the use of heat during the extraction process, which may result in a higher yield but can degrade some of the oil's beneficial components.
