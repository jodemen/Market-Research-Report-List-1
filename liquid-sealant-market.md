<p><p>The liquid sealant market is segmented into four types based on resin type, rubber type, oil modified type, and natural polymer type. Resin type sealants are made from synthetic resins that offer high strength and resistance to aging. Rubber type sealants are composed of rubber compounds, providing excellent elasticity and adhesion. Oil modified type sealants incorporate oils into their formulation for improved flexibility and durability. Natural polymer type sealants use plant-based materials that are eco-friendly and sustainable. These different types cater to varying application demands in industries such as construction, automotive, and electronics.</p></p>
