<p><p>Heart rate monitors (HRMs) are devices used to measure and monitor an individual's heart rate during physical activities. There are two main types of HRMs in the market: chest-strap HRMs and wrist-based HRMs. Chest-strap HRMs consist of a strap that is worn around the chest, with an electrode sensor that detects the electrical signals of the heart, providing accurate heart rate data. On the other hand, wrist-based HRMs are worn directly on the wrist and use optical sensors to measure heart rate through the pulsating blood flow.</p></p>
