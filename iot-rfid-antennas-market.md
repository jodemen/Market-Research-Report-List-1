<p><strong>The IoT RFID Antennas Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive RFID</li><li>Active RFID</li><li>Semi-Active RFID</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IoT RFID Antennas market consists of three types: Passive RFID, Active RFID, and Semi-Active RFID. Passive RFID antennas do not require power and rely on energy from the reader to transmit data. Active RFID antennas have their own power source and can send and receive signals independently. Semi-Active RFID antennas have a power source but only transmit signals when triggered by the reader. Each type caters to different needs and applications, offering varying ranges, power consumption levels, and data transmission capabilities for efficient communication in IoT systems.</p></p>
