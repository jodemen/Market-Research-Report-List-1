<p><p>Food and beverage casters come in two main types, swivel casters and rigid casters. Swivel casters have a rotating mechanism that allows for easier maneuverability and turning, making them ideal for applications where the cart needs to change direction frequently. Rigid casters, on the other hand, are fixed in place and only move back and forth, providing stability and support. Both types of casters are essential in the food and beverage industry for transporting carts, shelves, and equipment efficiently and safely.</p></p>
