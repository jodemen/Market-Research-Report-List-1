<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Capacitive Hygrometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Relative Type</li><li>Absolute Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Capacitive hygrometers are classified into two main types: Relative Type and Absolute Type. </p><p>Relative Type hygrometers measure humidity relative to the current temperature, providing a percentage value of the humidity level. They are commonly used in homes and offices to monitor indoor humidity levels.</p><p>Absolute Type hygrometers measure humidity in absolute terms, typically expressed in grams of water vapor per cubic meter of air. These are often used in industrial settings where precise humidity control is necessary, such as in pharmaceutical manufacturing or food storage facilities.</p></p>
