<p><strong>The Commercial Kitchen Knife Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Stamped Blade Products</li><li>Forged Blade Products</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial kitchen knives can be categorized into two main types based on their manufacturing process: stamped blade products and forged blade products. Stamped blade products are made by cutting the blade out of a large sheet of metal, resulting in a more affordable and lighter knife. On the other hand, forged blade products are made by heating and hammering a single block of metal to shape the blade, resulting in a more durable and heavier knife. Each type has its own advantages and is preferred by different chefs based on their needs and preferences.</p></p>
