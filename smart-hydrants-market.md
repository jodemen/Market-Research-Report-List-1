<p><p>Smart hydrants are categorized into two types in the market: Retrofit and One-piece. Retrofit smart hydrants are designed to be installed on existing traditional hydrants, transforming them into smart hydrants with advanced functionalities. On the other hand, One-piece smart hydrants are standalone units that come pre-equipped with smart technology and sensors. These units are designed to be installed as new hydrants, offering the benefits of smart technology right from the start. Both types cater to varying needs in the market for smart hydrant solutions.</p></p>
