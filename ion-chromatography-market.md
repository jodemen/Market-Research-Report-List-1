<p><strong>The Ion Chromatography Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ion Exchange Chromatography</li><li>Ion Exclusion Chromatography</li><li>Ion Pair Chromatography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ion chromatography is a technique used for separating and analyzing ions in a solution. The Ion Exchange Chromatography market involves the use of an ion exchange resin to separate ions based on their charge. Ion Exclusion Chromatography separates ions based on their size using a non-ionic stationary phase. Ion Pair Chromatography involves the formation of ion pairs between analyte ions and counter ions to separate them. These methods are employed in various industries like pharmaceuticals, environmental analysis, and food and beverage testing for accurate and efficient ion analysis.</p></p>
