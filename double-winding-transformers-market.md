<p><p>Double winding transformers, also known as dual winding transformers, are electrical devices used for transferring electrical energy between two or more circuits using electromagnetic induction. They consist of two separate windings, each connected to a different circuit. One winding, known as the primary winding, receives energy from an input source, while the other winding, called the secondary winding, delivers the transformed energy to the output circuit.</p><p>The market for double winding transformers has been experiencing steady growth in recent years.
