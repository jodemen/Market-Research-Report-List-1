<p><p>Liquid filled transformers are classified into different types based on the type of liquid used as insulation and coolant. The main types are seed oil filled transformers, mineral oil filled transformers, and others. Seed oil filled transformers use natural oils derived from plants as the insulating and cooling medium. Mineral oil filled transformers use refined mineral oils, such as naphthenic and paraffinic oils. Other types of liquid filled transformers may use synthetic oils or other types of liquids as the insulation and coolant.</p></p>
