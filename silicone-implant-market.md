<p><p>Silicone Implant Market Types include Silicone Filling and Saline Filling. Silicone Filling implants are filled with silicone gel, which provides a more natural look and feel compared to saline implants. These implants are popular for their durability and long-lasting results. Saline Filling implants, on the other hand, are filled with a sterile saltwater solution. While they are adjustable in size and can be easily removed, they may not feel as natural as silicone implants. Both types have their own advantages and are chosen based on individual preferences and medical advice.</p></p>
