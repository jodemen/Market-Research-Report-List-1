<p><p>Activated Carbon Fiber (ACF) is a highly porous material used in various applications such as air purification, water treatment, and medical devices. There are different types of ACF based on the raw material used in their production process. Pitch Based ACF is derived from coal tar pitch, while Polyacrylonitrile (PAN) Based ACF is made from synthetic polymer fibers. Viscose Staple Based ACF is produced from natural cellulose fibers. Other types of ACF can also be derived from alternative sources such as coconut shells or bamboo.</p></p>
