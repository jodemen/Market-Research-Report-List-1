<p><strong>The Power Toothbrush Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Vibration</li><li>Rotation-oscillation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The power toothbrush market can be divided into two main types: vibration and rotation-oscillation. Vibration toothbrushes use rapid back-and-forth movements to remove plaque and debris from teeth, while rotation-oscillation toothbrushes have brush heads that rotate in opposite directions. Vibration toothbrushes are typically more affordable and easy to use, while rotation-oscillation toothbrushes are often considered more effective at cleaning teeth and preventing gum disease. Both types offer advanced cleaning technology compared to manual toothbrushes.</p></p>
