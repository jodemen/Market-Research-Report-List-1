<p><p>SAR satellite services market types typically include L-band, C-band, and X-band offerings. L-band SAR satellites operate in the 1-2 GHz frequency range, providing low-resolution images suitable for broad coverage. C-band SAR satellites operate in the 4-8 GHz frequency range, offering moderate resolution imagery. X-band SAR satellites operate in the 8-12 GHz frequency range, providing high-resolution images ideal for detailed observations. Each market type caters to different needs and preferences, offering varying levels of image clarity and coverage capabilities.</p></p>
