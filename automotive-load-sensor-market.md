<p><p>Automotive Load Sensor market consists of various types including Wheel Spoke Sensor, Torque Transducer, Hole Sensor, and S-Sensor. Wheel Spoke Sensor measures load on individual wheel spokes, Torque Transducers measure the twisting force on a component, Hole Sensors detect load changes in a specific area, and S-Sensors monitor load distribution on a surface. Each type serves a specific purpose in automotive applications, providing accurate measurements and data to optimize performance and ensure safety.</p></p>
