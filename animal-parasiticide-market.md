Ectoparasiticides are a type of parasiticides used to control external parasites like ticks, fleas, and mites, which infest the skin and fur of animals. On the other hand, endoparasiticides are used to eliminate internal parasites such as worms and protozoa that affect the gastrointestinal tract or other internal organs.
