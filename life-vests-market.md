<p><p>Life vests market includes three main types: foam vests, inflatable vests, and hybrid vests. Foam vests are the most traditional option, providing buoyancy through the use of foam padding. Inflatable vests are more compact and comfortable as they inflate with the pull of a cord or automatically upon immersion in water. Hybrid vests combine the features of foam and inflatable vests, offering both comfort and reliability. Each type caters to different preferences and needs, ensuring that individuals can find the right life vest for their activities.</p></p>
