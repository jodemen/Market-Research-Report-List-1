<p><p>High frequency internal vibrators are used in construction for consolidating concrete. The market for these vibrators is mainly divided into two types: electric and pneumatic. Electric high frequency internal vibrators are powered by electricity and are commonly used for smaller projects. Pneumatic high frequency internal vibrators, on the other hand, are powered by compressed air and are typically suitable for larger construction projects. Both types offer efficient and effective concrete consolidation, but the choice between electric and pneumatic depends on the specific project requirements.</p></p>
