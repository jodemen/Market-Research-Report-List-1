<p><strong>The Emulsion Breaker Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Oil Soluble Demulsifying Agent</li><li>Water Soluble Demulsifying Agent</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Emulsion breakers, also known as demulsifiers, are used to separate oil and water in emulsions. The oil soluble demulsifying agents are designed to break oil-in-water emulsions, while water soluble demulsifying agents break water-in-oil emulsions. Oil soluble demulsifiers work by bonding with the oil molecules in the emulsion, causing them to separate from the water. Water soluble demulsifiers, on the other hand, bond with the water molecules in the emulsion to facilitate separation from the oil. Both types of emulsion breakers are essential in industries such as oil and gas, petrochemicals, and wastewater treatment.</p></p>
