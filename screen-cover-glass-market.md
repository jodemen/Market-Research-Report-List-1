<p><p>Screen Cover Glass Market Types include Synthetic Sapphire, Tempered Glass, and PMMA. Synthetic Sapphire offers the highest level of scratch resistance and durability, making it ideal for high-end devices. Tempered Glass provides excellent impact protection and scratch resistance while maintaining touch sensitivity. PMMA, or acrylic glass, is a more affordable option with decent scratch resistance but lower impact protection. Each type of screen cover glass caters to different needs and preferences, allowing consumers to choose based on their priorities and budget.</p></p>
