<p><ul><li>CCD Camera</li><li>CMOS Camera</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Replacement rear view mirrors with CCD camera use a charge-coupled device to capture high-quality images with low noise levels, making them ideal for low light conditions. On the other hand, CMOS camera mirrors utilize complementary metal-oxide-semiconductor technology to produce sharper images with lower power consumption. Each type of mirror caters to different consumer needs, with CCD cameras providing superior image quality in dim lighting, while CMOS cameras offer a more cost-effective and efficient solution.</p></p>
