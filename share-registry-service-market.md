<p><p>Type I market in share registry services refers to a market where only the original issuer of the shares can maintain their registry. This means that all shareholders are recorded on the issuer's registry. On the other hand, Type II market allows third-party companies to provide registry services for the shares, enabling shareholders to have their ownership details held by a separate entity. This gives shareholders more flexibility and options in managing their investments.</p></p>
