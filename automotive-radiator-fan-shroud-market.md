<p><p>Automotive radiator fan shroud market includes two main types - Ring Type and Box Type. Ring Type shrouds encircle the radiator fan within a circular structure, providing full coverage and efficient cooling. Box Type shrouds, on the other hand, feature a square or rectangular shape that contains the fan within a confined space. Both types play a crucial role in directing airflow and preventing overheating in the vehicle's cooling system. Customers can choose between these options based on their specific vehicle requirements and design preferences.</p></p>
