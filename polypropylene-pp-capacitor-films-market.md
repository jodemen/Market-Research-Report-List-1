<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) Capacitor Films are commonly categorized into two types based on their surface finish: Smooth PP film and Rough PP film. Smooth PP films are characterized by their uniform and sleek surface texture, making them ideal for applications requiring high dielectric strength and reliable electrical insulation. On the other hand, Rough PP films have a textured surface that enhances their mechanical strength and resistance to external environmental factors. Both types of PP capacitor films are widely used in various electronic components and equipment for their superior performance and durability.</p></p>
