<p><strong>The Water Jet Cutter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pure Waterjet</li><li>Abrasive Jet</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The water jet cutter market can be segmented into three types- pure waterjet, abrasive jet, and others. Pure waterjet cutters use high-pressure water streams to cut soft materials like rubber, foam, and plastic. Abrasive jet cutters, on the other hand, mix water with abrasive substances like garnet to cut harder materials like metal and stone. The "others" category includes hybrid waterjet systems that combine both pure water and abrasive cutting methods for varied applications.</p></p>
