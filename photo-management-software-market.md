<p><p>Photo management software market can be classified into two types: cloud-based and web-based. Cloud-based software allows users to store, organize, and edit their photos on remote servers accessed through the internet. This enables easy photo management and sharing, with the added benefit of remote access from any device. On the other hand, web-based software provides similar functionalities but is hosted on the user's own server or network.
