<p><p>Automotive Engine Mount Rubber market consists of three main types of rubber: Natural Rubber, Synthetic Rubber, and Vulcanized Rubber. Natural Rubber is derived from the latex of rubber trees, offering high elasticity and tear resistance. Synthetic Rubber is artificially produced to mimic the properties of natural rubber, with variations in durability and flexibility. Vulcanized Rubber undergoes a process that improves its strength and durability by linking polymer chains. Each type of rubber offers unique characteristics and benefits for use in automotive engine mounts.</p></p>
