<p><p>RF Cable Assemblies are categorized into three main types based on their flexibility: Semi-Rigid RF Cable Assemblies, Semi-Flexible RF Cable Assemblies, and Flexible RF Cable Assemblies. Semi-Rigid cables are rigid and provide excellent electrical performance, suitable for applications requiring tight bends. Semi-Flexible cables offer moderate flexibility while still maintaining good electrical performance. Flexible cables offer the highest flexibility, allowing for easy installation in tight spaces but with slightly lower electrical performance compared to semi-rigid and semi-flexible cables.</p></p>
