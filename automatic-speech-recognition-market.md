<p><ul><li>Speaker-Dependent Speech Recognition System</li><li>Speaker-Independent Speech System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) market includes two main types: Speaker-Dependent Speech Recognition System and Speaker-Independent Speech System. The Speaker-Dependent system requires users to train the system to recognize their unique speech patterns, limiting its usage to specific individuals. On the other hand, the Speaker-Independent system can recognize speech from any user without prior training, making it more versatile and widely applicable. These different market types within ASR cater to different needs and use cases in industries such as customer service, transcription services, and voice assistants.</p></p>
