<p><p>Silt barriers are used in construction and environmental projects to prevent the spread of silt and sediment. They are available in two main types: woven fibres and non-woven fibres. Woven fibre silt barriers are made of tightly woven fabric, providing a durable solution for controlling sediment. Non-woven fibre barriers are typically made of synthetic materials and are cost-effective for temporary erosion control. Both types are effective in preventing soil erosion and protecting water quality in construction sites and other areas prone to sediment runoff.</p></p>
