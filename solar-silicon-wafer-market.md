<p><ul><li>Single Crystal Silicon Wafer</li><li>Polycrystalline Silicon Wafer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Solar Silicon Wafer market consists of two main types: Single Crystal Silicon Wafer and Polycrystalline Silicon Wafer. Single Crystal Silicon Wafers are made from a single continuous crystal structure, providing higher efficiency and purity but at a higher cost. Polycrystalline Silicon Wafers, on the other hand, are made from multiple crystal structures, offering lower efficiency and purity compared to single crystal wafers but at a lower cost. Both types are widely used in the solar industry for the production of solar cells.</p></p>
