<p><p>Web POS software is available in different market types, including cloud-based and on-premise solutions. Cloud-based POS software operates on the internet and is accessed through a web browser. It eliminates the need for local installation and offers benefits like remote access and automatic updates. On-premise POS software, on the other hand, requires installation on local servers and is managed in-house. While it provides greater control and security, it requires ongoing maintenance and updates. Both market types offer distinct advantages and it depends on the business needs and preferences in choosing the right solution.</p></p>
