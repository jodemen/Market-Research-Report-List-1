<p><p>Ho doped fibers are used in various applications such as fiber lasers and amplifiers. The market types for these fibers include Single Clad Fiber, Double Clad Fiber, and Triple Clad Fiber. Single Clad Fiber has one layer of cladding surrounding the doped core, Double Clad Fiber has an additional inner cladding layer for improved pump efficiency, and Triple Clad Fiber has two additional cladding layers for even higher pump efficiency and reduced signal loss. Each type offers different benefits and is used in specific applications based on their design.</p></p>
