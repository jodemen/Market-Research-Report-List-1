<p><p>Garlic peeling machines are available in two main types - wet-type and dry-type. Wet-type machines use water to soften the garlic skin before peeling, while dry-type machines rely on mechanical methods to remove the skin without water. The wet-type machines are more suitable for larger scale industrial use, while dry-type machines are preferred for smaller scale operations or where water usage is a concern. Both types offer efficient and automated solutions for peeling garlic quickly and effectively.</p></p>
