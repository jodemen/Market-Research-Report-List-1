<p><p>Remote proctoring solutions are categorized into three types. Fully automated solutions employ artificial intelligence (AI) algorithms to monitor and detect any suspicious behavior during an online exam. Record and review solutions involve recording the entire exam session and allowing instructors to review the footage later for any irregularities. Fully live market solutions include a live proctor who monitors the exam session in real-time, ensuring integrity and preventing cheating. These remote proctoring solutions offer flexibility and convenience while maintaining the integrity of online assessments.</p></p>
