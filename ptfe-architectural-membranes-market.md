<p><strong>The PTFE Architectural Membranes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane</li><li>Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE Architectural Membranes are categorized into two types based on their water-repelling properties: Hydrophobic PTFE Membrane and Hydrophilic PTFE Membrane. Hydrophobic PTFE Membranes are designed to repel water, making them ideal for applications where water resistance is crucial, such as in roofing systems. On the other hand, Hydrophilic PTFE Membranes are designed to attract water, allowing for better breathability and moisture management, making them suitable for applications where moisture control is important, such as in facades or tension structures.</p></p>
