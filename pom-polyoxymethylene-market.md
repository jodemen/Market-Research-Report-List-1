<p><strong>The POM (Polyoxymethylene) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>POM-H</li><li>POM-C</li></ul></p>
<p><p>POM-H primarily refers to homopolymer grade polyoxymethylene, while POM-C refers to copolymer grade. POM-H has higher stiffness, strength, and hardness compared to POM-C, making it suitable for applications requiring high mechanical strength and rigidity. On the other hand, POM-C offers better chemical resistance and thermal stability, making it ideal for use in chemical and high-temperature environments. Both types of POM have a wide range of industrial applications, including automotive parts, consumer goods, and electrical components.</p></p>
