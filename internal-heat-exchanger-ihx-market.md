<p><p>Internal Heat Exchanger (IHX) systems are classified into two main types: double pipe IHX and others IHX. Double pipe IHX utilizes two pipes, one inside the other, for efficient heat transfer. On the other hand, other IHX types include shell and tube, plate heat exchangers, and finned tube IHX. These systems are designed for different applications based on their unique configurations and heat transfer capabilities. Both types play a crucial role in various industries for heating and cooling processes.</p></p>
