<p><p>Citric acid powder market is primarily divided into two types: Citric Acid Anhydrous and Citric Acid Monohydrate. Citric Acid Anhydrous is the crystalline form of citric acid that contains no water molecules, while Citric Acid Monohydrate is the form that contains one water molecule per citric acid molecule. Both types are widely used in food and beverage industry as acidulants, flavoring agents, and preservatives. They also have applications in pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, and household cleaning products.</p></p>
