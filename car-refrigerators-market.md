<p><p>The car refrigerator market can be classified into two main types: compressor type car refrigerators and electric cooler type car refrigerators. Compressor type car refrigerators use a traditional compressor system to cool the interior, similar to a household refrigerator. On the other hand, electric cooler type car refrigerators use a thermoelectric cooling system, which is more energy-efficient but may not cool items as quickly as compressor type models. Both types offer portable refrigeration solutions for keeping food and beverages cool while on the go.</p></p>
