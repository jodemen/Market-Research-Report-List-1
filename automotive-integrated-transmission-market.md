<p><p>Automotive Integrated Transmission market includes two main types of support systems, hydraulic and electronic. On the other hand, electronic support systems utilize sensors and control units to optimize gear shifts and increase fuel efficiency. Both systems aim to enhance overall driving experience and improve vehicle performance by efficiently managing power transmission within the vehicle. These technologies play a significant role in the development of modern automotive transmissions.</p></p>
