Dynamic imaging involves capturing real-time images of moving structures within the body, such as blood flow or joint movement. Static imaging, on the other hand, involves taking still images of internal structures for diagnostic purposes. Both types of imaging rely on X-ray detectors to capture high-quality images that help healthcare professionals diagnose and treat various medical conditions.</p></p>
