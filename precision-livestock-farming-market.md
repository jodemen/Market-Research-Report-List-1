<p><p>Precision Livestock Farming (PLF) is an advanced farming practice that utilizes various technologies and tools to monitor and manage livestock in real-time. This allows for quicker decision-making and better management of livestock.</p><p>Another trend is the development of precision feeding systems. By providing the right amount of feed based on individual requirements, precision feeding systems can optimize feed conversion efficiency and reduce waste.</p><p>The Precision Livestock Farming Market is also witnessing the adoption of remote monitoring and control systems.
