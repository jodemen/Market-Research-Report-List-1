<p><p>Repulping Aid Market types include Deinking Agent, Bleach, Dispersant, Surfactant, and Others. Deinking Agents are used to remove ink from recycled paper. Bleach is used to remove color and brighten the pulp. Dispersants help disperse fibers and prevent agglomeration. Surfactants are used to reduce surface tension and improve pulp drainage. Other types may include enzymes for breaking down contaminants or fillers for improving paper strength. These various aids play essential roles in the repulping process, ensuring high-quality recycled paper production.</p></p>
