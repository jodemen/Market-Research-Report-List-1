<p><p>PC-TV tuners are devices that allow computers to receive and display television signals. There are two main types of PC-TV tuners: USB tuners and TapC tuners. USB tuners are external devices that connect to a computer via USB port, offering flexibility and portability. On the other hand, TapC tuners are internal cards that are installed directly into the computer's motherboard, providing a more streamlined and integrated solution. Both types of tuners allow users to watch and record TV programs on their computers, enhancing the entertainment experience.</p></p>
