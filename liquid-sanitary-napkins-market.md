<p><p>Liquid sanitary napkins, also known as liquid absorbent sanitary napkins, are feminine hygiene products that are designed to provide better absorption and leakage protection during menstruation. These napkins contain a layer of superabsorbent polymer (SAP) that turns liquid into gel, ensuring maximum absorbency and preventing leakage.</p><p>The liquid sanitary napkins market has been steadily growing in recent years due to various factors. The increasing awareness about personal hygiene, growing preference for disposable sanitary products, and the rising demand for comfortable and leak-proof options are driving the market growth. Additionally, the development of innovative technologies and materials, such as the use of SAP, has further fueled the market expansion.</p><p>One of the latest trends in the liquid sanitary napkins market is the integration of organic and natural materials. Consumers are becoming more conscious about the environmental impact of conventional sanitary products, leading to a shift towards eco-friendly alternatives. Manufacturers are responding to this trend by introducing organic liquid sanitary napkins that are made from biodegradable and compostable materials.</p><p>Another emerging trend is the introduction of scented options in liquid sanitary napkins.
