<p><strong>The Low Profile Solenoids Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Flat Face</li><li>Conical Face</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low profile solenoids are available in two main types: flat face and conical face. Flat face solenoids have a straight, flat surface where the plunger meets the coil, allowing for a consistent contact area. Conical face solenoids, on the other hand, have a cone-shaped plunger that allows for a larger contact area when engaged. Each type has its own advantages depending on the specific application, such as providing a more even distribution of force in flat face solenoids or increased contact area in conical face solenoids.</p></p>
