<p><p>The civil aircraft stand entry guidance system market encompasses two types of systems: Visual Docking Guidance System (VDGS) and Advanced Visual Docking Guidance System (A-VDGS). </p><p>A Visual Docking Guidance System provides pilots with visual cues, such as lights or markings on the ground, to assist them in parking the aircraft accurately at the gate. This system improves safety and reduces the likelihood of collisions during parking.</p><p>On the other hand, an Advanced Visual Docking Guidance System offers more advanced features, such as automated aircraft-specific information, real-time data exchange with aircraft systems, and integration with ground handling systems. This system provides pilots with highly accurate guidance during the parking process, enhancing efficiency and reducing the risk of errors.</p></p>
