<p><strong>The Automotive HVAC Module Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Blowing Type</li><li>Duct Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive HVAC modules come in two main types - direct blowing type and duct type. Direct blowing type modules have air vents facing directly towards the passengers, providing more immediate and concentrated airflow. Duct type modules have air vents that distribute airflow through a series of ducts, allowing for more customized and flexible airflow options. Both types offer efficient heating, cooling, and ventilation solutions for vehicles, catering to different preferences and needs of passengers.</p></p>
