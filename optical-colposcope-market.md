<p><p>Optical colposcopes come in two main types: monocular and binocular. Monocular colposcopes have a single eyepiece, allowing the user to view the magnified image through one eye. Binocular colposcopes, on the other hand, have two eyepieces, enabling the user to view the image with both eyes simultaneously. The choice between the two types depends on the user's preference and comfort level. Both monocular and binocular colposcopes are used for magnified visualization of the cervix during colposcopy procedures.</p></p>
