<p><p>Gasoline Direct Injection (GDI) system market comprises of various types such as Turbulent Jet (T) system, Piezo Fuel Injector, and Water Jet System. Turbulent Jet (T) system injects fuel into the combustion chamber in a turbulent manner, enhancing mixing of air and fuel for efficient combustion. Piezo Fuel Injector uses piezoelectric crystals to control the fuel injection process with precision. Water Jet System injects a fine mist of water into the combustion chamber to cool it down, reducing emissions and improving fuel efficiency.</p></p>
