<p><p>Cultivation substrates play a crucial role in plant growth, with various types available in the market. Soilless mixes are popular for their nutrient-rich composition and ability to promote healthy root development. Composts are organic substrates made from decomposed materials, providing essential nutrients for plants. Gravel is a non-organic option that offers good drainage and aeration. Inert media like perlite or vermiculite provide structural support for plants. Other types of substrates include coco coir, peat moss, and rockwool, each offering unique benefits for plant growth.</p></p>
