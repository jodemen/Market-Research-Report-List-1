<p><p>Ferrite bonded magnets are classified into two main types: Injected Magnets and Pressed Magnets. Injected Magnets are made by injecting a mixture of ferrite powder and resin into a mold, while Pressed Magnets are created by compressing a mixture of ferrite powder and binder under high pressure. Both types offer strong magnetic properties and are commonly used in various applications such as motors, sensors, and magnetic assemblies. Each type has its unique manufacturing process and benefits, catering to different customer requirements.</p></p>
