<p><p>The cardiovascular agents market includes various types of medications used for the treatment of heart-related conditions. Antianginal agents help in reducing chest pain by improving blood flow to the heart. Antiarrhythmic agents are used to treat irregular heart rhythms. Inotropic agents enhance the force of heart contractions. Miscellaneous cardiovascular agents encompass a range of drugs used for various cardiovascular issues. Peripheral vasodilators widen blood vessels outside the heart. Renin inhibitors are drugs that lower blood pressure by blocking an enzyme called renin. Sclerosing agents help treat varicose veins. Vasodilators relax and widen blood vessels.</p></p>
