<p><p>Micro-ecological regulators comprise probiotics, prebiotics, and synbiotics, which play important roles in promoting gut health and overall well-being. Probiotics are live beneficial bacteria that can aid digestion and boost the immune system. Prebiotics are non-digestible fibers that serve as food for probiotics, helping them thrive in the gut. Synbiotics are a combination of probiotics and prebiotics, working together to improve the balance of beneficial bacteria in the gut. These types of micro-ecological regulators contribute to a healthy microbiome and improve overall health.</p></p>
