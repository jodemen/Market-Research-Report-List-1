<p><p>LED fog lamps are specialized lights used in vehicles to improve visibility in foggy or misty conditions by providing a focused beam of light that cuts through the fog. The rise in awareness about road safety and the need for better visibility during adverse weather conditions are driving the adoption of LED fog lamps.
