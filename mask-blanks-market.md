<p><ul><li>Low Reflectance Chrome-film Mask Blanks</li><li>Attenuated Phase Shift Mask Blanks</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low Reflectance Chrome-film Mask Blanks, typically made of chrome on glass substrate, have low reflectance properties that minimize image distortion caused by reflections. This type of mask blank is commonly used in advanced lithography processes. Attenuated Phase Shift Mask Blanks, on the other hand, contain a phase shifting material that helps to improve resolution and contrast in semiconductor manufacturing. These mask blanks are designed to reduce interference effects and enhance pattern definition on the semiconductor wafer during photolithography.</p></p>
