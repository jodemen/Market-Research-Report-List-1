<p><p>Contextual advertising is a type of targeted advertising that delivers ads based on the user's current context or situation. Activity-based advertising focuses on delivering ads relevant to the user's current online activities, such as their browsing history or search queries. Location-based advertising targets users based on their GPS or IP location, delivering ads specific to their current physical location. Other types of contextual advertising include demographic-based advertising which tailors ads to the user's demographics, and content-based advertising which matches ads to the specific content on a webpage or app.</p></p>
