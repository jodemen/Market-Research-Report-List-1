<p><p>Motorcycle Immobilizers market can be divided into two types based on their installation method: Installation Type and Non-Installation Type. Installation Type immobilizers are permanently integrated into the motorcycle's system during manufacturing or as an aftermarket upgrade. On the other hand, Non-Installation Type immobilizers are portable devices that can be easily attached and removed from the motorcycle. Both types serve the same purpose of preventing theft by immobilizing the motorcycle's engine or ignition system.</p></p>
