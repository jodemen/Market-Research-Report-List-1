<p><strong>The Automotive Telematics System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Embedded Type</li><li>Tethered Type</li><li>Integrated Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive telematics system market can be categorized into three types: embedded, tethered, and integrated. Embedded telematics systems are built-in and permanently installed in the vehicle, providing services like GPS navigation and vehicle diagnostics. Tethered telematics systems rely on an external device, like a smartphone, for connectivity and applications. They offer features such as hands-free calling and music streaming. Integrated telematics systems seamlessly integrate the vehicle's systems with connectivity services, offering advanced features like remote start, stolen vehicle tracking, and emergency services.</p></p>
