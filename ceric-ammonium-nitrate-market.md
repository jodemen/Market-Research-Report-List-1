<p><p>Ceric ammonium nitrate is used in two main market types: neutralization method and conversion method. In the neutralization method, ceric ammonium nitrate is produced by the reaction of cerium oxide with nitric acid. This method is typically used in smaller scale applications. In the conversion method, ceric ammonium nitrate is produced through the oxidation of cerous ammonium nitrate with hydrogen peroxide. This method is preferred for larger scale industrial applications due to its efficiency and cost-effectiveness.</p></p>
