<p><p>Vacuum pumps are essential in various industries for removing air and gas molecules from a sealed space. Dry vacuum pumps utilize a mechanism without the need for lubricating fluid, making them ideal for clean environments. Liquid ring vacuum pumps use a rotating liquid to create a vacuum, suitable for handling liquids and corrosive gases. Rotary vane vacuum pumps utilize rotating vanes to create a vacuum, making them versatile and efficient. Each type of vacuum pump offers unique benefits and is suited for different applications.</p></p>
