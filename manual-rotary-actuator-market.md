<p><p>Manual rotary actuators are classified into various types based on their functionality. Manual quarter-turn actuators are designed to rotate valves or dampers by 90 degrees. Manual multi-turn actuators are used for applications requiring multiple rotations to open or close valves. Manual part-turn actuators offer precise control over the degree of rotation needed. Other types of manual rotary actuators include linear actuators and rack and pinion actuators. Each type of actuator caters to specific industrial requirements and is used based on the application's needs and limitations.</p></p>
