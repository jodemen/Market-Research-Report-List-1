<p><p>The Industrial Cybersecurity Software market consists of two major types: On-Premise and On-Cloud. On-Premise refers to software that is installed and operated on the company's own infrastructure, providing complete control over data and security but requiring maintenance and upfront investments. On the other hand, On-Cloud software is hosted and managed by a third-party provider, accessible via the internet. It offers convenience, scalability, and cost-effectiveness, but may raise concerns regarding data privacy and reliance on external systems. Both options cater to different requirements and preferences of industrial organizations.</p></p>
