<p><p>Resistance welding consumables market includes various types of materials used in the welding process: stick electrodes, solid wires, flux cored wires, Submerged Arc Welding (SAW) wires, and fluxes. Stick electrodes are commonly used for general welding applications, while solid wires provide a stable arc and high deposition rates. Flux cored wires are used for high-speed welding and increased productivity. SAW wires are used for high-precision welding, and fluxes are used to protect the molten weld pool from impurities. Each type of consumable offers unique benefits for specific welding applications.</p></p>
