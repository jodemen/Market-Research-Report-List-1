<p><p>PVC Pressure Pipes market includes three main types: Chlorinated PVC Pipe, Unplasticized PVC Pipe, and Plasticized PVC Pipe. Chlorinated PVC Pipe is durable and can withstand higher temperatures and pressure, making it ideal for industrial applications. Unplasticized PVC Pipe is rigid and suitable for underground water distribution systems due to its resistance to corrosion and chemicals. Plasticized PVC Pipe is flexible and commonly used in plumbing and irrigation systems due to its ease of installation and low cost.</p></p>
