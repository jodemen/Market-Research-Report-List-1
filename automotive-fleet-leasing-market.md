<p><p>Automotive fleet leasing involves renting or leasing a large number of vehicles to companies or organizations for their business operations. <p><p>Automotive fleet leasing refers to the process of renting vehicles for a specified period. Open-ended market refers to a leasing agreement that gives flexibility by allowing the lessee to extend the lease term or purchase the vehicle at its residual value. This type suits businesses uncertain about asset needs. Close-ended market, on the other hand, is a fixed-term leasing agreement where the lessee returns the vehicle at the end of the contract. It appeals to businesses seeking predictable costs and replacement options.</p></p>
