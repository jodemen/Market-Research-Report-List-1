<p><p>Butyl adhesives come in various market types, with the most common being butyl adhesive tape and butyl adhesive paste. Butyl adhesive tape is a strip of adhesive material coated with butyl adhesive that is used for bonding, sealing, and insulating purposes. On the other hand, butyl adhesive paste is a thick adhesive material that is applied directly onto surfaces for bonding and sealing applications. Both types of butyl adhesives are known for their strong bonding capabilities, flexibility, and resistance to harsh conditions.</p></p>
