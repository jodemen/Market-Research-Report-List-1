<p><strong>The Quantum Dot QD Display Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>QDEF</li><li>QLED</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quantum Dot Display technology is divided into two main types: Quantum Dot Enhancement Film (QDEF) and Quantum Dot Light Emitting Diodes (QLED). QDEF uses a film containing quantum dots to enhance the colors and brightness of an existing display, while QLED involves directly integrating quantum dots into LEDs to create self-emitting displays. Both technologies offer improved color accuracy, brightness, and energy efficiency compared to traditional display technologies, making them popular choices for high-quality displays in various applications.</p></p>
