<p><p>Satellite Positioning Service is divided into two main market types: Precise Positioning Service (PPS) and Standard Positioning Service (SPS). PPS refers to a highly accurate and reliable positioning service used by government agencies and authorized users for critical applications like military operations and scientific research. On the other hand, SPS offers positioning services to the general public worldwide with slightly lower accuracy. SPS is commonly used for navigation, mapping, and location-based services in industries like transportation, agriculture, and consumer devices.</p></p>
