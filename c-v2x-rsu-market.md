<p><p>C-V2X RSU (Roadside Unit) market is segmented into three types - Single Mode, Dual Mode, and Multimode. Single Mode RSUs are equipped with only one communication mode, either LTE or ITS-G5. Dual Mode RSUs support both LTE and ITS-G5 communication simultaneously. Multimode RSUs can communicate via LTE, ITS-G5, and NR (New Radio) technology. Each type caters to different connectivity needs and interoperability requirements within the automotive industry, offering flexibility and compatibility with various communication protocols.</p></p>
