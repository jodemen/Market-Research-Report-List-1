<p><p>Tungsten CMP (Chemical Mechanical Planarization) slurries are used in the semiconductor industry for polishing and planarizing wafers during the manufacturing process. These slurries are available in two main types, micron and nano, depending on the particle size. Micron slurries have larger particles, typically between 1 to 10 microns, while nano slurries have smaller particles below 100 nanometers. Micron slurries are commonly used for rough polishing, while nano slurries are used for finer polishing and achieving higher levels of planarization in semiconductor devices.</p></p>
