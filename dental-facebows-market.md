<p><p>Dental facebows are used in dentistry to accurately transfer the position of the upper teeth and jaws to the articulator for precise and effective dental treatments. There are two main types of dental facebows: Arcon dental facebows and non-arcon dental facebows. Arcon dental facebows have a hinge connection that mimics the natural movement of the mandible, while non-arcon dental facebows do not have this feature. Both types are essential tools in dental practices for achieving precise occlusal relationships and improving treatment outcomes.</p></p>
