<p><p>Disposable microvascular clips are used in surgical procedures to occlude small blood vessels. They come in two main types: absorbable and non-absorbable. Absorbable clips are made of materials that dissolve over time, eliminating the need for removal and reducing the risk of complications. Non-absorbable clips, on the other hand, are made of materials that remain in the body permanently. The choice between absorbable and non-absorbable clips depends on the specific needs of the surgery and the patient's condition.</p></p>
