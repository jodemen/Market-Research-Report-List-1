<p><p>Pigmentation disorder treatment market is segmented into various types including melasma, vitiligo, albinism, and post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation. Melasma refers to dark patches on the skin, often caused by hormonal changes. Vitiligo is a condition where the skin loses its color, resulting in white patches. Albinism is a genetic disorder characterized by a lack of melanin pigment production, leading to light-colored hair, skin, and eyes. Post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation occurs after an injury or inflammation, causing dark spots on the skin. These different types require specific treatments and solutions tailored to address their respective pigmentation disorders.</p></p>
