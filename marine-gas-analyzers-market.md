<p><p>Marine gas analyzers can be classified into two main types: portable and fixed. Portable gas analyzers are small, lightweight devices that can be easily carried around and used for on-site gas analysis in various marine applications. Fixed gas analyzers, on the other hand, are installed permanently at specific locations on a ship or marine facility to continuously monitor gas levels in the surrounding environment. Both types of gas analyzers play a crucial role in ensuring safety and compliance with regulations in the marine industry.</p></p>
