<p><strong>The Capsule Coffee Machines Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Source System</li><li>Open Source System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Closed source system in capsule coffee machines refers to machines that are designed to only work with specific capsules made by the same brand. This limits consumer choice as they are restricted to using capsules from that particular brand. On the other hand, open source system machines allow consumers to use capsules from various brands, offering more flexibility and variety. This type of system promotes competition among capsule manufacturers and gives consumers the freedom to choose from a wider range of coffee options.</p></p>
