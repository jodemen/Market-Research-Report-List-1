<p><ul><li>Single-punch</li><li>Rotary</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medium speed tablet presses market encompasses two main types: single-punch and rotary presses. Single-punch presses are typically utilized for small-scale production and are manually operated with one punch and die set. Rotary presses, on the other hand, are designed for higher volume production and feature multiple dies mounted on a rotating turret. They are automated and can produce tablets at a faster rate compared to single-punch presses. Both types of presses play a crucial role in the pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries.</p></p>
