<p><p>Medical packaging paper market is divided into two main types: coated paper and uncoated paper. Coated paper is treated with a layer of chemicals or polymer to enhance its durability, moisture resistance, and printing quality, making it ideal for packaging medicines and medical devices. On the other hand, uncoated paper is untreated and offers a more natural look and feel, commonly used for packaging items that do not require as much protection. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and integrity of medical products.</p></p>
