<p><p>The Medical Instrument Holding Arm Market includes two main types: Articulated and Rail-mounted arms. Articulated arms have multiple joints that allow for a wide range of movement and flexibility in positioning medical instruments. These arms are commonly used in surgical procedures where precision and range of motion are critical. Rail-mounted arms are attached to a track system, offering stability and the ability to easily move instruments along the track. This type of arm is often used in operating rooms for convenient access to equipment.</p></p>
