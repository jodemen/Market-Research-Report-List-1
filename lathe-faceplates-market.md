<p><p>Disposable faceplates in the lathe market refer to faceplates that are intended for one-time use and are discarded after each job. These faceplates are typically made of lower-cost materials and are less durable than reusable faceplates. On the other hand, reusable faceplates can be used multiple times for various lathe operations. These faceplates are usually constructed from higher-quality materials to ensure longevity and accuracy in machining.
