<p><p>A base station is a radio transmitter/receiver that serves as the hub for connecting wireless devices to a telecommunications network. <p><p>Base station market can be categorized into single-band and multi-band types based on the frequency bands they support. Single-band base stations are designed to operate on a specific frequency band, providing coverage and capacity for a particular range of frequencies. Multi-band base stations, on the other hand, are capable of supporting multiple frequency bands simultaneously, offering greater flexibility and efficiency in managing network resources. Multi-band base stations are increasingly becoming popular due to their ability to support diverse communication standards and technologies in a single deployment.</p></p>
