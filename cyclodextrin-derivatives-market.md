<p><p>Cyclodextrin derivatives market types refer to different forms of cyclodextrin molecules that are modified to enhance their properties for various applications. HP-β-CD, SBE-β-CD, RM-β-CD, and HP-γ-CD are some common types. HP-β-CD has a hydroxypropyl group attached, SBE-β-CD has a sulfobutylether group, RM-β-CD has a randomly methylated group, and HP-γ-CD has a hydroxypropyl group in the gamma position. These derivatives offer improved solubility, stability, and bioavailability, making them valuable in pharmaceuticals, food, and other industries.</p></p>
