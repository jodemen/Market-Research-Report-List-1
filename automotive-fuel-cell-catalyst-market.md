<p><strong>The Automotive Fuel Cell Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heterogeneous Type</li><li>Homogeneous Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive fuel cell catalyst market can be categorized into two main types: heterogeneous type and homogeneous type. Heterogeneous type catalysts involve solid catalysts that are in a different phase from the reactants, providing a surface for the reaction to take place. On the other hand, homogeneous type catalysts involve catalysts that are in the same phase as the reactants, resulting in a more uniform reaction mixture. Both types of catalysts play crucial roles in promoting the chemical reactions necessary for efficient operation of fuel cells in vehicles.</p></p>
