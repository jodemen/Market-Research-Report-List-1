<p><p>Mothballs are commonly categorized into two main types: Synthetic Mothballs and Natural Mothballs. Synthetic mothballs are typically made from chemicals like naphthalene or paradichlorobenzene, which effectively repel moths and other insects. On the other hand, Natural mothballs are made from natural ingredients such as cedarwood or lavender, providing a more eco-friendly and non-toxic alternative to synthetic mothballs. Both types of mothballs are used to protect clothing and other items from moth damage by repelling the insects.</p></p>
