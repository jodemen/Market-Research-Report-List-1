<p><ul><li>Inactive Siloxane</li><li>Active Siloxane</li><li>Modified Polysiloxane</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone products for fiber and textiles market can be categorized into three main types: Inactive siloxane, active siloxane, and modified polysiloxane. Inactive siloxane products are mainly used as softeners and lubricants for textiles. Active siloxane products are utilized for imparting properties like water repellency and stain resistance to fibers and textiles. Modified polysiloxane products are custom-designed silicone molecules that offer specific functionalities such as flame retardancy or antimicrobial properties. Each type plays a crucial role in enhancing the performance and durability of textile materials in various applications.</p></p>
