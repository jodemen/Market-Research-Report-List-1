<p><p>Retail casters market offers two main types - Swivel Casters and Rigid Casters. On the other hand, Rigid casters have a fixed direction and are best suited for straight-line movement. Both types come in various sizes, materials, and load capacities to meet different retail industry needs. Swivel casters are popular for shopping carts and display racks, while rigid casters are commonly used for equipment and furniture that require stability.</p></p>
