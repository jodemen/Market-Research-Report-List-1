<p><p>A Gallium Nitride Transistor, also known as GaN transistor, is a type of power semiconductor device that uses gallium nitride as the semiconductor material. GaN transistors offer advantages such as higher power density, faster switching speeds, and lower on-state resistance compared to traditional silicon-based transistors. Due to these benefits, GaN transistors are increasingly being used in applications such as power supplies, wireless charging, electric vehicles, and RF amplifiers.</p><p>The Gallium Nitride Transistor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7% during the forecast period.
