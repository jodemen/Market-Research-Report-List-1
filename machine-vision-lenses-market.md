<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Machine Vision Lenses Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>C-Mount</li><li>CS-Mount</li><li>F-Mount</li><li>V-Mount</li><li>T2-Mount</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Machine vision lenses market includes various types such as C-Mount, CS-Mount, F-Mount, V-Mount, T2-Mount, and others. C-Mount lenses are the most common with a 1" diameter thread and widely used in industrial applications. CS-Mount lenses have a smaller back focal length and are suitable for smaller sensors. F-Mount lenses are compatible with high-resolution sensors. V-Mount lenses are used for applications requiring high magnification. T2-Mount lenses have a 42mm thread and are commonly used in astronomy. Other lenses include unique mounting options for specialized applications.</p></p>
