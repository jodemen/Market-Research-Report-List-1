<p><p>Pump casting refers to the manufacturing process of creating pump components from various materials. Silical sol casting involves using a water glass solution to create the mold, which is then filled with molten metal. Sand casting uses a mixture of sand and a binder to form the mold, and the molten metal is poured into it. Die casting involves injecting molten metal into a mold under high pressure. These casting methods are used in the pump market to produce high-quality, durable components for pumps.</p></p>
