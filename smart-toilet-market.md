<p><strong>The Smart Toilet Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Smart Toilet Seat</li><li>Integrated Smart Toilet</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Smart Toilet market can be categorized into two main types: Smart Toilet Seat and Integrated Smart Toilet. Smart Toilet Seats are attachments that can be added to existing toilets to provide smart features such as bidet functions, heating, and automatic lid opening. Integrated Smart Toilets, on the other hand, come as a complete unit with all the smart features built into the toilet itself, including sensors, remote control operation, and personalized user settings. Both types cater to the increasing demand for smart home technology in the bathroom.</p></p>
