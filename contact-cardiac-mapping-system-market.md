<p><strong>The Contact Cardiac Mapping System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electroanatomical mapping</li><li>Basket catheter mapping</li><li>Traditional endocardial catheter mapping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The contact cardiac mapping system market consists of three types: electroanatomical mapping, basket catheter mapping, and traditional endocardial catheter mapping. Electroanatomical mapping involves using catheters to create a detailed, three-dimensional map of the heart's electrical signals and anatomical structures. Basket catheter mapping utilizes a balloon-like device with multiple electrodes to collect electrical data from within the heart. Traditional endocardial catheter mapping involves inserting catheters into the heart to detect electrical signals and map its inner surfaces. These mapping systems help physicians diagnose and treat various cardiac conditions.</p></p>
