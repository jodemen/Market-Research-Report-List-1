<p><p>Lacquer thinner is a type of solvent used to thin lacquer-based paints, varnishes, and stains. There are two main types of lacquer thinners: mineral spirits and oil of turpentine. Mineral spirits are derived from petroleum and are less flammable than oil of turpentine, which is a natural product derived from pine trees. While both types of lacquer thinners are effective for thinning lacquer products, mineral spirits are generally more commonly used due to their lower cost and wider availability.</p></p>
