<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical Hydraulic</li><li>Electrohydraulic</li><li>Electric Assist</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electric Power Steering Control System market includes three main types: Mechanical Hydraulic, Electrohydraulic, and Electric Assist. Mechanical Hydraulic systems use a hydraulic pump driven by the engine to assist with steering. Electrohydraulic systems combine hydraulic power with electronic control for more precise steering. Electric Assist systems use an electric motor to provide steering assistance, offering improved fuel efficiency and design flexibility. Each type has its own advantages and is used in different vehicles based on their requirements and performance characteristics.</p></p>
