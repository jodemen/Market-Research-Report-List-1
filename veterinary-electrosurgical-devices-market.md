<p><strong>The Veterinary Electrosurgical Devices Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Monopolar Electrosurgical Devices</li><li>Bipolar Electrosurgical Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Veterinary Electrosurgical Devices Market includes Monopolar and Bipolar devices. Monopolar devices use a single electrode for cutting or coagulating tissues, with the electrical current returning through a dispersive electrode placed on the patient's body. Bipolar devices have both active and return electrodes within the instrument tip, allowing for precise tissue manipulation without the risk of stray currents affecting surrounding tissues. Each type of device has its own advantages and applications in veterinary surgery, catering to a wide range of surgical needs.</p></p>
