<p><ul><li>Hydraulic Brake Hose</li><li>Vacuum Brake Hose</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automobile brake hoses are divided into three main types: hydraulic brake hose, vacuum brake hose, and others. Hydraulic brake hoses use fluid pressure to transfer force from the brake pedal to the brake calipers, providing effective braking power. Vacuum brake hoses use vacuum pressure to assist in braking and are commonly found in older vehicles. The "others" category includes various types of brake hoses that may have unique designs or materials. Each type plays a critical role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of an automobile's braking system.</p></p>
