<p><p>In the Chinese E-Learning market, there are two main types: On-Premise and Cloud-Based. On-Premise refers to a system where the software and hardware required for E-Learning are installed and managed on the client's own servers. This gives the client more control and flexibility but requires higher initial investments in infrastructure. On the other hand, Cloud-Based E-Learning systems operate remotely through internet servers, allowing users to access content anytime and anywhere. This type offers scalability, affordability, and ease of use since all maintenance and updates are handled by the provider.</p></p>
