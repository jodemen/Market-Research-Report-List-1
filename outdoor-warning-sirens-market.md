<p><p>Outdoor warning sirens come in three main types: Directional, Rotating, and Omni-Directional. Directional sirens emit sound waves in a specific direction, allowing for targeted alerts. Rotating sirens have a rotating mechanism that emits sound in all directions, ideal for covering large areas. Omni-Directional sirens emit sound in all directions simultaneously, providing maximum coverage. Each type has its own advantages and is used based on the specific needs of the area being covered.</p></p>
