<p><p>Fullerene nanotubes market consists of different types based on the number of walls in the nanotubes. Single-wall nanotubes (SWNT) have a single layer of carbon atoms, providing unique mechanical and electrical properties. Double-wall nanotubes (DWNT) contain two layers of carbon atoms, offering enhanced strength and conductivity. Multi-wall nanotubes (MWNT) have multiple layers, providing a balance between strength and flexibility. Each type of nanotube has specific applications in industries such as electronics, aerospace, and materials science.</p></p>
