<p><strong>The Petroleum Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FCC Catalysts</li><li>Hydrotreating Catalysts</li><li>Hydrocracking Catalysts</li><li>Catalytic Reforming Catalysts</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The petroleum catalyst market can be divided into four main types: FCC catalysts, which are used in fluid catalytic cracking processes to convert heavy hydrocarbons into lighter products; hydrotreating catalysts, which are used to remove sulfur, nitrogen, and other impurities from crude oil; hydrocracking catalysts, which are used to break down larger molecules into smaller ones; and catalytic reforming catalysts, which are used to convert low-octane naphtha into high-octane gasoline. Other types of catalysts may also be used in various refining processes within the petroleum industry.</p></p>
