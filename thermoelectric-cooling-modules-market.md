<p><p>Thermoelectric Cooling Modules are devices used to cool electronic components through the Peltier effect. These modules are categorized into two types: Single Stage and Multi Stage. </p><p>Single Stage modules consist of a single pair of Peltier elements, offering a simple and cost-effective cooling solution. Multi Stage modules, on the other hand, contain multiple pairs of Peltier elements, providing enhanced cooling efficiency and the ability to reach lower temperatures. Both types cater to different cooling requirements and are widely used in various industries.</p></p>
