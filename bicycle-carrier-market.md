<p><p>The bicycle carrier market can be divided into three main types: rear & hitch bike racks, roof mounted bike racks, and others. Rear & hitch bike racks attach to the rear of the vehicle or hitch receiver, making them convenient and easy to access. Roof mounted bike racks are secured on the top of the vehicle, providing additional storage space and leaving the rear accessible. The "others" category includes unique designs such as trunk mounted racks or spare tire-mounted racks, offering a variety of options for transporting bicycles.</p></p>
