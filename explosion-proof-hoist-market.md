<p><p>Explosion-proof hoists are designed for use in hazardous environments where the risk of explosions is present. There are two main types of explosion-proof hoists: chain hoists and wire rope hoists. Chain hoists use a system of interlocking metal links to lift and lower heavy objects, while wire rope hoists utilize a strong steel cable wound around a drum. Both types of hoists are specially engineered to prevent sparks or overheating that could potentially cause an explosion in volatile surroundings.</p></p>
