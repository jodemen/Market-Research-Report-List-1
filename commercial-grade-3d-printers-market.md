<p><strong>The Commercial Grade 3D Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>FDM Technology</li><li>SLA Technology</li><li>SLS Technology</li><li>DMLS Technology</li><li>3DP Technology</li><li>SLM Technology</li><li>EBM Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The commercial grade 3D printer market consists of various types of technologies. FDM (Fused Deposition Modeling) technology is the most widely used, using melted thermoplastic to create objects layer by layer. SLA (Stereolithography) technology uses photopolymerization to solidify liquid resin into precise shapes. SLS (Selective Laser Sintering) technology uses lasers to fuse powdered materials together. DMLS (Direct Metal Laser Sintering) technology melts metal powder with lasers to create metal objects. 3DP (3D Printing) technology uses a binding agent and powdered material to create objects. SLM (Selective Laser Melting) technology melts metal powder to produce metal components, while EBM (Electron Beam Melting) technology uses an electron beam to melt metal powder for manufacturing.</p></p>
