<p><p>Radiofrequency Ablation Generators come in two main types: Monopolar and Multi-polar Fashion. In Monopolar Fashion, the radiofrequency energy is delivered through a single electrode to a target tissue, while in Multi-polar Fashion, multiple electrodes are used to deliver energy simultaneously to the tissue. Both types of generators are used in medical procedures to create heat and destroy abnormal tissue, such as in the treatment of tumors or cardiac arrhythmias. The choice of generator type depends on the specific medical procedure being performed.</p></p>
