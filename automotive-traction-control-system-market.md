<p><strong>The Automotive Traction Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mechanical System</li><li>Hydraulic System</li><li>Electric System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive Traction Control Systems can be categorized into three main types: Mechanical System, Hydraulic System, and Electric System. Mechanical systems use mechanical devices such as limited-slip differentials to control wheel slippage. Hydraulic systems use hydraulic actuators to regulate power distribution to the wheels. Electric systems rely on sensors and electronic control units to monitor wheel speed and adjust power accordingly. Each type of system offers unique benefits and features to improve vehicle stability and performance in various driving conditions.</p></p>
