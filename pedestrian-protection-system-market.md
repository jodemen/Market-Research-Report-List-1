<p><strong>The Pedestrian Protection System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Passive Pedestrian Protection System</li><li>Active Pedestrian Protection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Passive Pedestrian Protection Systems are designed to reduce the severity of injuries in the event of a collision by absorbing impact energy and minimizing the risk of pedestrian contact with the vehicle's hard surfaces. Examples include external airbags and energy-absorbing bumpers. Active Pedestrian Protection Systems, on the other hand, use sensors and cameras to detect pedestrians in the vehicle's path and automatically apply brakes or steer away to avoid a collision. Both systems are aimed at improving pedestrian safety on the road.</p></p>
