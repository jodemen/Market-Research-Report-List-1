<p><p>Dental mouth openers are instruments used by dentists to keep a patient's mouth open during dental procedures. There are two main types: mouth prop and mouth gag. A mouth prop is a device that is placed inside the patient's mouth to maintain the open position. It provides support and reduces strain on the jaw muscles. On the other hand, a mouth gag is a tool that is placed around the patient's mouth to keep it open. This allows better access to the oral cavity and facilitates dental treatments.</p></p>
