<p><p>The Chinese herbology market comprises two primary types of medicines: Chinese Patent Medicine and Chinese Herbal Medicine. Chinese Patent Medicine refers to herbal formulas that have been processed and packaged into pills, capsules, or powders for easy consumption. These medicines are standardized and have a fixed formula. On the other hand, Chinese Herbal Medicine includes raw herbs or herbal formulas that are prepared by blending multiple herbs together. These medicines are typically customized based on individual patient needs and are prepared by a trained herbalist.</p></p>
