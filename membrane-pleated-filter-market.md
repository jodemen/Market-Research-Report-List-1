<p><p>Membrane pleated filters are available in different types such as PP (polypropylene), PES (polyethersulfone), PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), and Nylon. These filters are widely used in various industries for their unique properties and applications. PP filters are known for their chemical compatibility and high dirt holding capacity. PES filters offer superior flow rates and low protein binding. PTFE filters are resistant to harsh chemicals and high temperatures. Nylon filters are durable and resistant to organic solvents. Each type caters to specific filtration needs and requirements.</p></p>
