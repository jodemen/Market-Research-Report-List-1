<p><ul><li>Open Heeled Fins</li><li>Closed Heeled (Full Foot) Fins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Scuba diving fins come in two main types: open-heeled fins and closed-heeled (full foot) fins. Open-heeled fins are designed with an adjustable strap that wraps around the back of the heel, allowing for a secure and customizable fit. These fins are typically worn with dive boots for added comfort and warmth. In contrast, closed-heeled fins feature a foot pocket that encloses the entire foot, eliminating the need for dive boots. Both types of fins offer unique benefits and cater to different preferences among divers.</p></p>
