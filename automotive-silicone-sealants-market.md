<p><ul><li>Single Component Silicone Sealant</li><li>Double Component Silicone Sealant</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Single component silicone sealants are ready to use and do not require any mixing before application. They are ideal for quick and easy sealing applications. Double component silicone sealants, on the other hand, require two separate components to be mixed together before application. This type of sealant offers higher strength and durability compared to single component sealants. Single component sealants are more convenient for small projects, while double component sealants are more suitable for larger and heavy-duty sealing applications.</p></p>
