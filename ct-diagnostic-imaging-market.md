<p><strong>The CT Diagnostic Imaging Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional CT scanners</li><li>Cone beam CT scanners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Conventional CT scanners are widely used in hospitals and imaging centers for general diagnostic purposes. They utilize a fan-shaped x-ray beam to create cross-sectional images of the body. On the other hand, Cone beam CT scanners are specifically designed for dental and maxillofacial imaging. They use a cone-shaped x-ray beam to produce high-resolution 3D images with lower radiation exposure. Both types of CT scanners play a crucial role in medical imaging, providing detailed information for accurate diagnosis and treatment planning.</p></p>
